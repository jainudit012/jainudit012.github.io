Our Mission

To position India as a leader in science and engineering. This is not because I think India is special. No country is, for that matter. But, because in my opinion, every country or community and its people should work extremely hard and contribute its share of effort if we are wanting to push our civilization to new and interesting frontiers.

Right now India is mostly a consumer of technology that others have invented and engineered. This world can be served better if this population of a billion and still growing, gets transformed into creators of hi-tech. India has enormous potential to bring positive change for everyone.

Anub Sinha - CEO
